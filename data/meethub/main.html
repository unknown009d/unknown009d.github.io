MeetHub

Plan. Meet. Repeat.

Event finding app where people find their interested events curated to their liking to meet people of their own types.

April 2025 - May 2025

Have you ever struggled to find the perfect place to be around?

Presenting you a platform which brings you the ability to find and manage events to curate your need and find people aligned with your interest.

User Research

This event management platform connects people based on shared interests by enabling the discovery, creation, and hosting of both in-person and virtual events.

Users can find events that match their preferences or organize their own gatherings. The platform simplifies event management with features like digital ticketing and a seamless check-in experience using QR.


User pain points

Event Discovery

Users struggle to find events relevant to their specific interests due to basic or generic filtering options.

Event Creation

New hosts are unsure how to structure an engaging event listing (title, images, tags, description).

Payments & Trust

Users are unsure about refund policies, additional fees, or what happens in case of event cancellation.

Form-factor UX flaws

Event creation on mobile feels cramped or confusing due to form layout.

Refining Design

After the usability test I figured out that I've overlooked in few of the user experience flows which could be improved with slight adjustments discussed below :

Problem #1

If you see the high fidelity design, we can see that it is similar to something we use daily like YouTube, reddit, X where we subconsciously use the app like we use the others.

Using Gestalt principal I've decided to make events like a card which enables to make the experience similar as other apps.

Problem #2

With the users having issues with creating their own event, I had a redesign for the lo-fi mockup creating a intuitive step-by-step process of creating an event.

Problem #3

That happens many a times where FOMO hits and you book something else like a wrong date or a wrong place, the process of changing those require customer support through mails or calls.

This can be solved with a simple button and a progress bar which can be cancelled if wrong decision has been made.
